About Us
FoodOverflow is a question and answer website for enthusiast food lovers. It first started as a final project for an open source software class by 2 university students in South Korea in 2022. It features questions and answers on a wide range of topics related to cooking including baking.

Inspired by Stack Overflow, the website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions anything related to the culinary arts. Users of Food Overflow can earn points through active participation in replying other users' questions with helpful answers. Users with the most points will be listed in the 'Top Contributors' list.
