FAQ
What is FoodOverflow?

FoodOverflow is a question and answer website for enthusiast food lovers.

What topics can I ask about here?

Stack Overflow is for professional and enthusiast food lovers, or anyone who just loves to cook. If your question generally covers…

- a specific cooking technique problem, or
- a kind of recipe, or
- tips or tricks or
- cooking utensils; and is
- a practical, answerable problem that is unique to culinary arts

…then you’re in the right place to ask your question!
You can ask any questions related to cooking such as tips, do's and don'ts and even suggestions. Not limited to only cooking, even questions about baking are welcomed!

How do I ask a question?

Click on the (+ Ask a Question) button on the main page. Type in your question title and description. Click (+ Post Question) and just wait for other users to reply your question.

Can I answer my own question?

Of course! If you already have the answer to your previously posted question, and you would like to document that knowledge in public so that others (including yourself) can find it later, it's perfectly okay to ask and answer your own question.

What is the 'Top Contributor' list?

The 'Top Contributor' lists the most active users that accumulated a number of points from contributing in replying to questions with helpful answers.

How do I earn points?

You need to actively participate in replying to questions asked by other users. If the reply is liked by other users, you will be awarded points for each like.
